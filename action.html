Send to each legislator
Can't find your legislators automatically? Visit Michigan Legislature to find your state rep and senator by address, then use the Copy button below to paste your message into an email to them directly.
After editing the subject or body, click Refresh links before sending.

Click the legislator button first — it will try to open your email automatically. If nothing opens, use one of the options below.

Easiest option — After clicking the legislator button, if AOL didn't open, click Copy message, go to mail.aol.com, start a new email, paste your message, and type the legislator's address in the To field.
Set up automatic opening — Go to mail.aol.com in Edge and sign in. Look for a bar asking "Allow mail.aol.com to open mailto links?" and click Allow.
Test it — Click a legislator button again. AOL Mail should open with the message pre-filled.
Easiest option — Click the legislator button, then click Yahoo Mail in the panel that opens. Yahoo will open in your browser with the message pre-filled.
Set up automatic opening — Go to mail.yahoo.com in Edge, sign in, and click Allow when asked to open mailto links.
Test it — Click a legislator button. Yahoo Mail should open with the message pre-filled.
No setup needed — Click the legislator button, then click Gmail in the panel that opens. Gmail opens in your browser with the message pre-filled.
That is it — Review the message and click Send.
Outlook desktop — Clicking the legislator button should open Outlook automatically if it is installed. If not, click Outlook web in the panel instead.
Outlook web — Click the legislator button, then click Outlook web. Opens in your browser with the message pre-filled. No setup needed.
Test it — Click a legislator button and choose Outlook web. Review and click Send.
Still stuck? — Click Copy address next to the legislator, open your email manually, start a new message, paste the address, then click Copy message and paste the body in.
