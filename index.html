Make Your Voice Heard for Michigan's Veterans

Michigan law fails to recognize commissioned officers of the U.S. Public Health Service and NOAA as veterans — even though federal law does. Four House bills would fix that. Two more (HB 5456 & HB 5457) would create a hyperbaric oxygen therapy pilot program for Michigan veterans — they passed the House and now need the Senate. Choose an issue below, tell us who you are, and we'll write a personalized letter to your legislators in under two minutes.
